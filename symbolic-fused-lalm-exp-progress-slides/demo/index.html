Symbolic-Aware Large Audio Language Models

Interactive Experiment Demo

Demo-style companion page for the Overleaf presentation. It follows the task-by-task organization of the UniAudio2.0 demo, but keeps our current claim boundary explicit: S_plan is the learned symbolic planning token; S_LLM is planned and not yet trained.

UniAudio2 Demo Paper Slides HTML
Current token S_plan

Learned symbolic planning token from MIDI-derived structure. Evaluated through reconstruction, acoustic-token proxy, stream controls, and MIR recovery.

Planned token S_LLM

LLM-supervised symbolic tokenizer/token stream. Not trained yet; should follow EXP031A controls.
